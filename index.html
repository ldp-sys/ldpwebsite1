About Us

Lighthouse Development Partners (LDP) was co-founded by childhood friends Harley Sisler and Sean Smith, who reconnected through their shared passion for real estate development. With distinct yet complementary backgrounds, they have positioned LDP as a formidable force in the real estate development sector.

Development Thesis

Our approach blends innovative design, economic feasibility, and a commitment to long-term impact. Drawing from our team's expertise in multifamily and hospitality development, finance, and market analysis, we focus on creating projects that are not only financially viable but also contribute positively to communities. We leverage cutting-edge technologies and sustainable practices, ensuring our developments thrive across market cycles.

Investment Criteria

At LDP, we focus on projects with strong fundamentals, growth potential, and alignment with our mission of sustainable development. Our expertise in navigating complex financing structures, particularly in the Low Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) space, enables us to bring forward multifamily and affordable housing projects that meet market needs and provide strong returns for our stakeholders.
